We Are Kuku-Pizza

Kuku-Pizza is a small local pizzeria located in Sligo, Ireland. We are proud to serve fresh and delicious pizzas to students, locals, and visitors in the area.

Our shop is located near ATU Sligo, which makes it the perfect place for students to grab a quick and tasty meal between lectures or after a long day of studying.

At Kuku-Pizza we focus on simple recipes, fresh ingredients, and friendly service.
